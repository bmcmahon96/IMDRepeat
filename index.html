Overview

This project aims to discover the rift between consumer opinion and critic reviews on game titles and genres in the video game industry, if it is present. There has often been a clash of opinon on certain video game titles between consumer and critics of the industry that has led to controversies over such disagreement. This project seeks to validate if their is a major gap between these two often times disparate groups in terms of opinion or if it is simply amplified due to controversy overtaking approval.

Analysis

Discoveries on this topic

  • Critic and Consumer consensus relatively similar
  • Global sales are trending downward
  • Large gap between consumer and critic consensus on Miscellaneous genre games when measuring by mean
  • The Sports genre remains the most well approved genre with both measures

Conclusions

From studying this topic, I have come to be surprised by the results. I had started this project with the belief that I would find a large rift between consumer and critic opinion. This may be down to my own bias after following controversies that split both consumer and critic opinion. From the data shown, these controversies do not represent the industry at a macro scale.

I also question the accuracy of the game sales figures as I theorize that the decline in sales globally shown through these statistics is inaccurate as I suspect that digital sales are not taken into account. The sheer drop off in the North American and European region's sales does not seem to reflect reality as the industry is going from strength to strength.

Critic and Consumer consensus relatively similar
Through analysis of each video game genre, it was found that consumer and critic rating of game titles remains overly consistent with each other.
Global sales are trending downward
Sales in each region studied have been trending downward. This may be caused by these statistics only tracking physical sales of game copies while the industry moves towards digital sales.
Large gap between consumer and critic consensus on Miscellaneous genre games when measuring by mean
This may be due to games in this category varying in quality and themes that may resonate with the consumer but impress critics less so and vice versa.
The Sports genre remains the most well approved genre with both measures
Sports genre titles tend to retain consistency over iterations with the main areas of improvement coming from graphical fidelity. This tends not to be as polarizing.
